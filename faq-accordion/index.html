FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, the majority of Frontend Mentor's challenges are free. There are some premium challenges that require a Pro subscription to access, but it will say on each challenge whether they are free or premium.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Absolutely! Frontend Mentor encourages you to use your completed projects in your portfolio. This is a great way to showcase your skills and experience to potential employers.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

Frontend Mentor has a supportive community of developers who are always happy to help. You can post your questions on the community forum, or join one of the many channels on the Frontend Mentor Discord server.
